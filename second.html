About Us

At Coffee Home, we believe that coffee is more than just a drink—it’s an experience. Nestled in the heart of Calatrava, Negros Occidental, Philippines, we have created a cozy and inviting space where coffee lovers can unwind, connect, and savor the perfect cup. Our goal is to offer an authentic coffee experience in a warm and welcoming atmosphere. Whether you're here for a morning pick-me-up, a casual conversation, or a moment of peace, our carefully crafted brews and comfortable ambiance make Coffee Home your go-to coffee spot. Step in, take a seat, and let the aroma of freshly brewed coffee make you feel right at home. ☕✨
